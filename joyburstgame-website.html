How to Play

  1. Start the game - Click the play button in the game frame above to begin your adventure.
  2. Move your character - Use the WASD keys or arrow keys to navigate around the map.
  3. Attack enemies - Your character attacks automatically when enemies are in range.
  4. Collect experience gems - Defeat monsters to collect experience points that allow you to level up.
  5. Choose upgrades - When you level up, select from various upgrades to enhance your character.
  6. Survive as long as possible - The game gets progressively harder; see how long you can last!

Pro Tip: Focus on movement and positioning. Staying still for too long will quickly get you surrounded by monsters!
